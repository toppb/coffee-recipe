# Dak Coffee Roasters Strawberry Kiss

Tags: Aeropress, Pourover
Brewer: Aeropress, Hario V60
Grinder: Hario Slim Hand Grinder (Aeropress), Niche Zero (Pourover)
Rating: ★★★★★
Number: coffee-bag-17

### Tasting Notes

- Strawberry
- Fruity
- Apricot

## Pourover

### Recipe

- 18g beans
    - 44 grind setting
- 240g water

### Technique

- Pour 30g of water
- Stir the ground and wait 30 seconds
- Pour 70g water and wait until 60 secs
- Pulse pour until 240g total
- Stop at 3:00

## Aeropress

### Recipe

- 14g beans
    - 5 grind setting (from the top)
- 200g water

### Technique

- Pour 200g water quickly
- Wait for 2:00 mins
- Swirl Aeropress
- Wait 30 seconds
- Press down with even force
