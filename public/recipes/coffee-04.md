## Pourover

### Recipe

- 21g beans
    - 11 grind setting (from the top)
- 240g water

### Technique

- Pour 30g of water
- Stir the ground and wait 30 seconds
- Pour 70g water and wait until 60 secs
- Pulse pour until 240g total
- Stop at 3:30
