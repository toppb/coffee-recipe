# Timbertrain Cosmic Candy

Tags: Batch, Espresso, Pourover
Brewer: Gaggia Classic Pro OPV Mod, Hario V60, Technivorm Moccamaster KBGT
Grinder: Hario Slim Hand Grinder, Niche Zero
Rating: ★★★★★
Number: coffee-bag-06

### Tasting notes

- Tart
- Fruity

## Espresso

### Recipe

- 18g beans
    - 11.25 grind setting
- 29 secs

## Pourover

### Recipe

- 18g beans
    - 6 from top (Hario)
- 240g water

### Technique

- Pour 30g of water
- Stir the ground and wait 30 seconds
- Pour 70g water and wait until 60 secs
- Pulse pour until 240g total
- Stop at 3:00
